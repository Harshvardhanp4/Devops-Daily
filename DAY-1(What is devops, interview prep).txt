---------------------------------------DEVOPS DAY 1---------------------------------------


1) What is Devops?
=> Devops is a process that improves/ increases organizations ability to deliver their applications. Devops is a process of improving the application delivery.

By ensuring :- 
 
I. Improving delivery.
II. Automation.
III. Quality.
IV. Monitoring.
V. Testing.

2) Why Devops?
=> To deploy any application 10 y before there were sys admin, build and release engineer(BRE), server admin. (This was manual work and took a lot of days). Hence to improve
this Devops was introduced. Devops improves efficiency by those above things.

3) How to introduce myself?
=> I am a Devops/Full Stack engineer: I am passionate about devops I have experience as a devops engineer . I worked previously as a backend engineer.

Roles and Responsibilities:
In the current org I take care of automation, I ensure quality I am maintaining for my applications, I ensure that I setup continuous monitoring, I have automated testing in this devops lifecycle.
